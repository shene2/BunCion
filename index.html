Expert Advice

Bunny Care Tips

🥬

Proper Diet

Hay should be 80% of your bunny's diet. Fresh leafy greens and limited pellets complete the balance.

🚫

Avoid Sugary Foods

Too much sugar can harm your bunny's sensitive gut. Fruits are treats, not staples!

💧

Fresh Water Daily

Change your bunny's water every single day. A clean water bottle prevents bacterial build-up.

🏃

Daily Exercise

Bunnies need at least 3 hours of free roaming daily to stay healthy and happy.

🩺

Vet Check-ups

Annual vet visits catch issues early. Find a rabbit-savvy exotic vet near you.

🫂

Gentle Bonding

Let your bunny come to you. Earn trust slowly through calm, low-to-the-ground interactions.
